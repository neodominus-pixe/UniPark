The Problem

Campus parking is a daily headache

Every morning, the same chaos — blocked cars, no way to reach owners, and security stretched thin.

01

Cars blocking each other

Students park in rows with no coordination, trapping cars for hours with no way out.

02

No way to contact the owner

There is no system to find out whose car is blocking you or how to reach them.

03

Security overloaded

Guards spend valuable time playing middleman between students instead of securing the campus.

04

Time wasted

Students miss classes waiting for a blocker who could have moved in seconds if contacted.

The Solution

Self-service parking in three steps

UniPark gives every student a role in keeping the lot running smoothly — no hardware, no app install, just a browser.

I

Register on arrival

Students enter their name, plate number, and estimated departure when they park. Takes under 30 seconds on a phone.

II

Find your blocker instantly

Enter your plate and UniPark shows you exactly who is blocking you — with their name, plate, and a one-tap call button.

III

Security stays in control

Admins get a live dashboard with every parked car, search filters, and the ability to mark any car as exited.
